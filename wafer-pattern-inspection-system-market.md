<p><strong>The Wafer Pattern Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Inspection System</li><li>Bright-Field Inspection System</li><li>Dark-Field Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer pattern inspection systems are used in the semiconductor industry to detect defects on wafers during the manufacturing process. There are three main types of wafer pattern inspection systems: Electron Beam Inspection System, Bright-Field Inspection System, and Dark-Field Inspection System. </p><p>Electron Beam Inspection System uses a focused beam of electrons to scan the wafer surface and detect defects at a high resolution. Bright-Field Inspection System illuminates the wafer with uniform lighting to identify defects based on changes in brightness. Dark-Field Inspection System uses angled lighting to detect defects by capturing scattered light.</p></p>
