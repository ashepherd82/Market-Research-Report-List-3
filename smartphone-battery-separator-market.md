Bilayer separators have two layers, offering enhanced durability and better thermal stability. The "others" category includes various advanced separator designs and materials that do not fit into the monolayer or bilayer classifications. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safety and performance of smartphone batteries.</p></p>
