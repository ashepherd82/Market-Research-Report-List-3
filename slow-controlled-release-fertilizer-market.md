<p><p>Slow & Controlled Release Fertilizer market includes Binary Compound Fertilizers and NPK Compound Fertilizers. Binary Compound Fertilizers are made up of two nutrient elements, providing a balanced supply of nutrients over time. NPK Compound Fertilizers contain a combination of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in varying ratios, allowing for tailored nutrient release rates. Both types offer gradual nutrient release, reducing the risk of nutrient leaching and improving overall plant uptake efficiency. These fertilizers are ideal for sustained plant growth and are widely used in agriculture and horticulture.</p></p>
