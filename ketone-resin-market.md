<p><p>Ketone Resin is classified into three main types based on its chemical structure - Carbon Chain Polymer, Hetero Chain Polymer, and Element Organic Polymer. Carbon Chain Polymers are made up of repeating carbon units, Hetero Chain Polymers contain other elements like oxygen or nitrogen in their structure, and Element Organic Polymers combine both carbon and other elements. These different types offer a range of properties and applications in various industries including adhesives, coatings, and printing inks.</p></p>
