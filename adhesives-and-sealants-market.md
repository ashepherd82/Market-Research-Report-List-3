<p><ul><li>Self-etching Adhesive</li><li>Selective-etching Adhesive</li><li>Total-etching Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesives and Sealants market can be categorized into three types based on the etching process they utilize: Self-etching Adhesive, Selective-etching Adhesive, and Total-etching Adhesive. Self-etching Adhesives do not require a separate etching step as they simultaneously etch and prime the tooth surface. Selective-etching Adhesives selectively etch only certain parts of the tooth surface. Total-etching Adhesives involve the etching of the entire tooth surface before application. Each type has its unique advantages and is used in different dental applications based on the requirements.</p></p>
