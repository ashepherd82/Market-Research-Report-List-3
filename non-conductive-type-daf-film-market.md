<p><p>Non-conductive type DAF film market can be divided into two main types: DAF (Direct Anodic Bonding) and DDAF (Direct Dielectric Bonding). DAF involves bonding materials directly to a silicon wafer using anodic bonding techniques, while DDAF involves bonding materials to a wafer using dielectric bonding techniques. Both types of films are non-conductive, which makes them suitable for use in various electronic applications where conductivity is not desired. These films offer excellent insulation properties and are widely used in the semiconductor industry for manufacturing electronic devices.</p></p>
