<p><strong>The 3D Sensor Array Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time of Flight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Structured Light and Time of Flight are two types of 3D sensor arrays used in various industries such as automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics. Structured Light technology works by projecting a pattern onto an object and measuring the distortion of this pattern to generate a 3D image. Time of Flight technology measures the time it takes for a light signal to bounce back from an object, allowing for accurate depth perception. Both technologies offer high precision and reliability, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
