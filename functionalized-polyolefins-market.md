<p><p>Functionalized Polyolefins market can be divided into four types based on the method of functionalization: Randomly Functionalized Polyolefins, where functional groups are randomly distributed along the polymer chain; End Functionalized Polyolefins, where functional groups are located only at the ends of the polymer chain; Block Functionalized Polyolefins, where functional groups are concentrated in specific blocks within the polymer chain; and Graft Functionalized Polyolefins, where functional groups are attached to the polymer chain via side chains.
