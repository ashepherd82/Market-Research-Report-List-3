<p><p>Magnetic track brakes are braking systems that use magnetic force to stop or slow down moving vehicles or objects on a track. There are two main types of magnetic track brakes - electromagnetic track brakes and permanent magnet track brakes. Electromagnetic track brakes use an electric current to generate magnetic force, while permanent magnet track brakes utilize fixed magnets to create the braking force. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as railways, roller coasters, and conveyor systems for safe and efficient braking operations.</p></p>
