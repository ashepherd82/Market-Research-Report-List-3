<p><ul><li>Reactive acrylic adhesive (SGA or UV adhesive)</li><li>Anaerobic Adhesives</li><li>Cyanoacrylate adhesive</li><li>Epoxy adhesive</li><li>Silicone adhesive</li><li>Polyurethane Adhesives (solvent-free reactive PU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineering adhesives are used in various industries for bonding applications. Reactive acrylic adhesives, such as SGA or UV adhesive, cure when exposed to UV light or a specific accelerator. Anaerobic adhesives cure in the absence of air, locking and sealing components. Cyanoacrylate adhesives bond quickly and are ideal for materials like rubber and plastics. Epoxy adhesives provide strong bonding for metals and composites. Silicone adhesives offer flexibility and resistance to high temperatures. Polyurethane adhesives, specifically solvent-free reactive PU, provide excellent bonding for a wide range of materials.</p></p>
