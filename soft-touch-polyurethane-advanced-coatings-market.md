<p><p>Soft Touch Polyurethane Advanced Coatings are available in two main types in the market: Water-Based and Solvent-Based. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, as they have lower levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and are easy to clean up with water. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings have higher VOC levels and require specialized solvents for cleanup. Both types offer different advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements such as durability, finish, and environmental impact.</p></p>
