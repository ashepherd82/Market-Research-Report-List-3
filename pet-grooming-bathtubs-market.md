On the other hand, free-standing bathtubs are stand-alone units that do not require installation. They offer flexibility and mobility, allowing groomers to easily move and position the bathtub as needed. Both types cater to the needs of pet groomers, but the choice depends on factors such as space availability and personal preference.</p></p>
