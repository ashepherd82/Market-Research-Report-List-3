<p><strong>The Fiber Laser Coding System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Fiber Laser System</li><li>Solid State Fiber Laser System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber laser coding systems are categorized into two main types: pulse fiber laser systems and solid state fiber laser systems. Pulse fiber laser systems work by rapidly pulsing the laser beam to mark materials with high precision and speed. On the other hand, solid state fiber laser systems use a solid-state medium to generate the laser beam, providing consistent and reliable coding capabilities. Both types of fiber laser systems are widely used in various industries for marking, engraving, and coding applications.</p></p>
