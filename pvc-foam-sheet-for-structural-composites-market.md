<p><ul><li>Open Cell</li><li>Closed Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC foam sheet for structural composites is available in two main types: Open cell and Closed cell. Open cell foam sheets have interconnected pockets of air, providing flexibility and cushioning properties. They are commonly used in applications requiring sound or vibration absorption. Closed cell foam sheets have sealed pockets of air, offering higher strength and water resistance. They are often utilized in marine and outdoor applications. Both types of foam sheets are lightweight, durable, and versatile for various structural composite projects.</p></p>
