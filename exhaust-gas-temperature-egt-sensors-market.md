<p><p>Exhaust Gas Temperature (EGT) sensors are used in automotive systems to measure the temperature of the exhaust gases. There are two main types of EGT sensors - PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) sensors and NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) sensors. </p><p>PTC sensors have a resistance that increases with temperature, while NTC sensors have a resistance that decreases with temperature. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in monitoring and controlling the performance and emissions of vehicles, ensuring optimal engine efficiency and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
