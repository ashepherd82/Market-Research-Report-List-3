<p><ul><li>Public Cloud</li><li>Private Cloud</li><li>Hybrid Cloud</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud-based integration platform market types include public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud.</p><p>Public cloud refers to a cloud solution where the integration platform is hosted and managed by a third-party service provider. It offers scalability, cost-efficiency, and easy accessibility, as resources are shared among multiple clients.</p><p>Private cloud is dedicated to a single organization and managed in-house or by a third-party. It provides greater control, security, and customization options but may involve higher costs.</p><p>Hybrid cloud integrates both public and private cloud infrastructures, allowing organizations to leverage the benefits of both, ensuring flexibility, scalability, and cost optimization.</p></p>
