<p><p>Anti-migraine drugs market comprises of three main types: Triptans, Ergots, and Others. Triptans are the most commonly used drugs for treating migraines as they help reduce inflammation and constrict blood vessels in the brain. Ergots work by narrowing blood vessels to relieve migraine symptoms. Other types of drugs include NSAIDs, opioids, and antiemetics, which are used for treating migraines with different mechanisms of action. Overall, these drugs are essential for managing and alleviating migraine symptoms in patients.</p></p>
