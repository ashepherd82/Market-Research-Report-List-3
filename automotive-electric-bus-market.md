<p><p>The automotive electric bus market consists of three main types: battery electric bus, plug-in hybrid electric bus, and fuel cell electric bus. Battery electric buses are fully powered by electric motors and rely on rechargeable batteries. Plug-in hybrid electric buses have both electric motors and a conventional engine, using a combination of both to operate. Fuel cell electric buses use hydrogen fuel cell technology to generate electricity and power the vehicle. These different types provide alternative solutions for sustainable and environmentally friendly public transportation options.</p></p>
