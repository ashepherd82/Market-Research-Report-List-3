<p><p>Sand control equipment refers to the tools and systems used in the oil and gas industry to prevent sand production and control wellbore stability. The market for sand control equipment can be divided into various types. Gravel pack involves placing gravel around the wellbore to reduce sand production. Fracturing pack utilizes fracturing techniques to treat the formation and improve sand control. Sand control screens are permeable devices that filter sand particles from the production fluids. Additionally, other types of sand control equipment include chemical treatments, re-usable sand screens, and cased hole gravel packs.</p></p>
