<p><ul><li>Stick Electrodes</li><li>Solid Wires</li><li>Flux Cored Wires</li><li>SAW Wires & Fluxes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Welding consumable material market consists of various types including stick electrodes, solid wires, flux cored wires, SAW wires, and fluxes. Stick electrodes are commonly used for general welding applications. Solid wires are used for MIG welding, while flux cored wires provide higher deposition rates. SAW wires are used for submerged arc welding, offering high productivity. Fluxes are used in combination with other consumables to protect the weld pool. Each type serves specific welding needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
