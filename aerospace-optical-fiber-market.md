<p><p>The Aerospace Optical Fiber Market consists of two main types: Single Mode and Multi Mode fibers. Single Mode fibers have a small core size and are designed for long-distance transmission with low signal attenuation. Multi Mode fibers have a larger core size and are used for short-distance applications within aircraft systems. Both types of fibers play a crucial role in providing high-speed and reliable communication within aerospace environments, ensuring efficient data transmission and connectivity for various aviation applications.</p></p>
