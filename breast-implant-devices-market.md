<p><p>Breast implant devices are medical prosthetics used to enhance the size, shape, and contour of a woman's breasts through surgical procedures. Manufacturers are increasingly focusing on developing innovative products made from biocompatible materials, ensuring durability, and providing natural-looking results. There is a rising demand for cohesive silicone gel-filled breast implants, known as "gummy bear implants," due to their ability to retain shape and minimize potential leakage risks.
