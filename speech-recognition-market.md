<p><strong>The Speech Recognition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Speaker Dependent</li><li>Speaker Independent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Speech recognition market can be categorized into two types - speaker dependent and speaker independent. In speaker dependent systems, the technology is trained to recognize and understand the speech patterns and characteristics of a specific user. This type of system provides accurate results for that particular individual but may struggle with unfamiliar users. On the other hand, speaker independent systems are designed to understand speech from any user, without prior training. Although they may be less accurate compared to speaker dependent systems, they offer more flexibility and usability for a wider range of users.</p></p>
