<p><p>Graphite electrode nipples are available in two main market types based on the number of threads per inch (TPI): 3 TPI and 4 TPI. The 3 TPI market offers electrodes with coarser threads, providing better resistance to thermal shock and superior mechanical strength. On the other hand, the 4 TPI market offers electrodes with finer threads, allowing for easier installation and removal. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the graphite electrode industry.</p></p>
