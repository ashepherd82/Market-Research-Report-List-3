<p><p>Web offset inks are used in printing processes where the ink is transferred from a plate to a rubber blanket before being pressed onto the printing surface. Coldset inks are used in web offset printing processes that do not require heat to dry the ink, making them ideal for newspapers and other publications. Heatset inks, on the other hand, are used in web offset printing processes that require heat to dry the ink quickly, making them suitable for magazines, catalogs, and other high-quality printed materials.</p></p>
