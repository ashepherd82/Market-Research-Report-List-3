<p><ul><li>Surface Mount High Frequency Relays</li><li>Through Hole High Frequency Relays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Frequency Relays are classified into two main types based on their mounting method: Surface Mount High Frequency Relays and Through Hole High Frequency Relays. Surface Mount High Frequency Relays are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, allowing for a more compact and streamlined design. Through Hole High Frequency Relays are mounted by inserting their leads through holes in the circuit board, providing a secure connection and stability. Both types serve the purpose of switching high frequency signals in various applications.</p></p>
