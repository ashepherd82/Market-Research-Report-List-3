<p><p>Propane water heaters are available in two main types: tankless propane water heaters and tank propane water heaters. Tankless propane water heaters heat water on demand, providing a continuous supply of hot water without the need for a storage tank. Tank propane water heaters, on the other hand, store hot water in a tank for later use. Each type has its own set of advantages and disadvantages, depending on factors such as space availability, energy efficiency, and cost.</p></p>
