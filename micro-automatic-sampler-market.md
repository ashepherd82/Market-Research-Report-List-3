<p><p>Micro Automatic Sampler market consists of various types, including Inhaled Type, Pusher Type, and Monolithic Sample Ring Type. Inhaled Type samplers are designed to capture particles directly from the air. Pusher Type samplers use a piston to push the sample into the collection chamber. Monolithic Sample Ring Type samplers have a rotating ring that collects samples from different positions. Each type caters to specific sampling needs and industries, providing efficient and reliable solutions for collecting samples in various environments.</p></p>
