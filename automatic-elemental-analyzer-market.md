<p><p>Automatic Elemental Analyzer Market Types include GC Chromatography, Frontal Chromatography, and Adsorption-Desorption. The GC Chromatography type involves separating and analyzing compounds in a gas phase. Frontal Chromatography is used for determining the concentration of specific components in a sample by comparing retention times. Adsorption-Desorption market type is based on the process of molecules adhering to a solid surface and then being released. Each type offers unique benefits and applications for accurately analyzing elemental composition in various samples.</p></p>
