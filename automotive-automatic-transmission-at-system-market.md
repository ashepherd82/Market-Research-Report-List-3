<p><p>Automotive Automatic Transmission (AT) System includes three main types - Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT), Hydraulic Automatic Transmission (AT), and Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT). CVT offers seamless gear shifting for better fuel efficiency; Hydraulic AT uses hydraulic fluid to transfer power from the engine to the wheels smoothly; DCT utilizes two clutches for almost instantaneous gear changes, providing a balance of performance and efficiency. Each type caters to different driving preferences and needs in the automotive market.</p></p>
