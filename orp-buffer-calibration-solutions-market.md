<p><p>ORP buffer calibration solutions come in two main types: Quinhydrone solutions and pre-made stabilized ORP solutions. Quinhydrone solutions are made by combining quinhydrone with a solution of known pH to create a reference electrode for ORP measurements. Pre-made stabilized ORP solutions are ready-to-use solutions that do not require any additional mixing or preparation. Both types of solutions are essential for calibrating ORP meters and ensuring accurate measurements in various industries such as water treatment, food and beverage, and research.</p></p>
