<p><p>Neurodiagnostic and monitoring devices are used to monitor and diagnose neurological conditions. The Electroencephalogram (EEG) market focuses on monitoring brain activity, while the Electromyography (EMG) market tracks muscle activity. Transcranial Doppler devices are used to assess blood flow in the brain. Intracranial pressure monitors provide information on pressure within the skull. Cerebral oximeters measure oxygen levels in the brain. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing conditions such as epilepsy, stroke, and traumatic brain injury.</p></p>
