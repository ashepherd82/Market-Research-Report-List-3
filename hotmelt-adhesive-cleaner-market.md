<p><p>Thermoplastic Hotmelt Adhesive Cleaner is used for cleaning hotmelt adhesives that become soft and pliable when heated. This type of cleaner is typically used for applications where the adhesive needs to be reapplied multiple times. Pressure Sensitive Hotmelt Adhesive Cleaner, on the other hand, is used for cleaning adhesives that are designed to bond with minimal pressure. These cleaners are specifically formulated to effectively remove pressure sensitive adhesives without damaging the substrate. Both types cater to different adhesive requirements and applications.</p></p>
