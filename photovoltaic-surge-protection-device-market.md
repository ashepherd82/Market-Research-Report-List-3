<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Surge Protection Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type 1 (Cope Direct Strike)</li><li>Type 2 (Limits Over Voltages)</li><li>Type 1+2 (Complete Protection)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Type 1 surge protection devices are designed to protect against direct lightning strikes. They are installed at the main electrical panel to prevent damage from high energy surges. Type 2 devices, on the other hand, protect against over voltages caused by indirect lightning strikes or switching actions. Type 1+2 devices offer complete protection by combining the features of both Type 1 and Type 2 devices. This ensures comprehensive protection for photovoltaic systems against all types of electrical surges.</p></p>
