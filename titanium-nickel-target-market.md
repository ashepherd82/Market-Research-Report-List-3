<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium Nickel targets have various market types, including plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are generally used in static sputtering applications, where the target remains stationary during the deposition process. They are suitable for producing thin films on large flat substrates. On the other hand, rotating targets are utilized in dynamic sputtering processes, where the target spins during the deposition. This type of target is often chosen when uniformity and film thickness control across the substrate surface are critical. Both plane and rotating targets cater to specific customer needs in the field of thin film deposition.</p></p>
