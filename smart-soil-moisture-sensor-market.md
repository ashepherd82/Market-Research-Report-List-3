<p><strong>The Smart Soil Moisture Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Soil Moisture Sensors</li><li>Soil Water Potential Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volumetric Soil Moisture Sensors measure the amount of water in the soil by determining the volume percentage of water in the soil. These sensors are used to provide accurate data on soil moisture levels, ensuring proper irrigation practices. On the other hand, Soil Water Potential Sensors measure the force exerted by water within the soil, providing information on how easy or difficult it is for plants to extract water. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in efficient water management and optimizing crop yield.</p></p>
