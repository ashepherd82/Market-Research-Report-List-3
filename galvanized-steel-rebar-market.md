<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Galvanized Steel Rebar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li><li>Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized steel rebar is available in two main types: Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar and Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar. Conventional hot-dip galvanized rebar involves immersing the steel in a bath of molten zinc, providing a protective coating. Continuous hot-dip galvanized rebar is coated using a continuous process, resulting in a more uniform and durable finish. Both types offer corrosion protection, extending the lifespan of the rebar in various construction applications. These options provide flexibility for different project requirements.</p></p>
