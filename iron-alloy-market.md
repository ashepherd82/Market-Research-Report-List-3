<p><strong>The Iron Alloy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Carbon</li><li>Medium Carbon</li><li>Low Carbon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron alloy market is categorized into three main types based on carbon content: high carbon, medium carbon, and low carbon. High carbon iron alloys contain more than 0.6% carbon, providing high strength and hardness, suitable for applications in tools and machinery parts. Medium carbon iron alloys contain 0.3% to 0.6% carbon, offering a balance between strength and ductility, commonly used in automotive components. Low carbon iron alloys have less than 0.3% carbon, providing good weldability and machinability, often used in construction and structural applications.</p></p>
