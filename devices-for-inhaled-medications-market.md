<p><p>The devices for inhaled medications market consists of various types such as dry powder inhalers (DPIs), metered-dose inhalers (MDIs), nebulizers, and soft mist inhalers. DPIs deliver medication in a fine powder form, which the patient inhales using their own breath. MDIs release a measured dose of medication in aerosol form using a propellant. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a mist that the patient inhales through a mask or mouthpiece. Soft mist inhalers produce a slow-moving mist of medication, allowing for easy inhalation.</p></p>
