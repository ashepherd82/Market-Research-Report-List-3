<p><p>The Aerospace Wind Tunnel Testing Services market consists of two main types of wind tunnels: vertical wind tunnel and horizontal wind tunnel. Vertical wind tunnels allow objects to float in mid-air by generating an upwards wind flow. These are mainly used for recreational purposes, such as indoor skydiving. On the other hand, horizontal wind tunnels generate a horizontal wind flow that simulates the conditions faced by aircraft during flight. These wind tunnels are primarily utilized for testing and improving aerodynamics and flight performance of aircraft and spacecraft.</p></p>
