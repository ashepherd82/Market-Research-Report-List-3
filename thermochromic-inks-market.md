<p><p>Thermochromic inks are specialty inks that change color in response to temperature variations. There are three main types of thermochromic inks in the market: Cold Activated, Touch Activated, and Warm Activated. Cold Activated inks change color when exposed to cold temperatures, Touch Activated inks respond to the heat from human touch, and Warm Activated inks change color when exposed to warm temperatures. These inks are used in various applications such as labels, packaging, and security features.</p></p>
