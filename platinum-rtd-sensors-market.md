<p><strong>The Platinum RTD Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platinum RTD sensors are commonly available in two types: wire-wound RTDs and thin film RTDs. Wire-wound RTDs are made by winding a platinum wire around a core, providing high accuracy and durability in extreme temperatures. Thin film RTDs, on the other hand, consist of a thin layer of platinum deposited onto a substrate, offering faster response times and better stability. Both types are widely used in various industries for temperature measurement applications, but each has its own unique characteristics and benefits.</p></p>
