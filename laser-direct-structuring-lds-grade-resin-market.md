<p><p>Laser Direct Structuring (LDS) Grade Resin Market includes various types such as PC, PC/ABS, PA/PPA, LCP, PBT, ABS, and others. PC (Polycarbonate) offers high impact strength and heat resistance. PC/ABS combines the toughness of ABS with the heat resistance of PC. PA/PPA (Polyamide) provides excellent mechanical properties. LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) is renowned for its high strength and chemical resistance. PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate) offers good electrical insulation. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is known for its impact resistance and dimensional stability. Other resin types include POM, PPO, and others.</p></p>
