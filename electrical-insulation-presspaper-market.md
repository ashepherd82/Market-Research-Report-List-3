<p><p>Electrical insulation presspaper is classified into three main types based on thickness: below 0.8 mm, 0.8-5.0 mm, and above 5.0 mm. Presspapers below 0.8 mm are typically used for applications where thin insulation is required, such as in electronic devices. Presspapers ranging from 0.8-5.0 mm are commonly used in transformers and motors for medium-level insulation. Presspapers above 5.0 mm are utilized in heavy-duty electrical equipment that requires thick insulation to withstand high voltages and temperatures. Each type caters to specific needs in different electrical applications.</p></p>
