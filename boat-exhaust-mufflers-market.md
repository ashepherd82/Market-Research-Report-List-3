<p><p>Boat exhaust mufflers come in various types, including chambered exhaust mufflers, straight through exhaust mufflers, and others. Chambered exhaust mufflers use chambers and baffles to reduce noise and improve airflow. Straight through exhaust mufflers have a simple design with perforated tubes to minimize back pressure. Other types may include hybrid mufflers or adjustable mufflers. Each type offers different levels of noise reduction and performance benefits, catering to the diverse needs of boat owners.</p></p>
