<p><p>Rail car positioners are equipment used to move rail cars in a predetermined position. There are two main types of rail car positioners in the market - Rack-Pinion positioners and Rope-Drawn positioners. Rack-Pinion positioners utilize a gear system to precisely position rail cars, while Rope-Drawn positioners use ropes or cables for movement. Both types allow for efficient and accurate positioning of rail cars in various industrial applications, such as loading and unloading processes in warehouses or manufacturing plants.</p></p>
