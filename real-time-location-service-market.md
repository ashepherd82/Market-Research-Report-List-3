<p><strong>The Real-Time Location Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tags or Signal Transmitter</li><li>Wi-Fi Networks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Real-Time Location Service (RTLS) market consists of two main types: tags or signal transmitters and Wi-Fi networks. Tags or signal transmitters are devices that are attached to an object or carried by a person, and they emit a signal that can be tracked in real-time. This allows for precise location tracking and monitoring. On the other hand, Wi-Fi networks provide location data based on the signals emitted by Wi-Fi access points. This method also enables real-time tracking and is often used in indoor environments where GPS signals may not be available.</p></p>
