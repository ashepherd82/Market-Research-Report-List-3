<p><p>High-pressure die casting machines are used for producing parts with tight tolerances and high repeatability. There are two main types of machines: cold chamber and hot chamber. In cold chamber machines, the metal is melted in a separate furnace and then transferred to the machine for casting. Hot chamber machines have a built-in melting pot, making them more efficient for metals with low melting points. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics for manufacturing complex metal components.</p></p>
