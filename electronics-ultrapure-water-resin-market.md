Mixed Resins are a combination of anion and cation resins, used to effectively remove impurities in ultrapure water. Anion Resins are specifically designed to remove negatively charged ions, while Cationic Resins are designed to remove positively charged ions. Each type of resin plays a crucial role in the purification process of ultrapure water used in the electronics industry for various applications.</p></p>
