<p><ul><li>Reversible</li><li>Irreversible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat indicating paint comes in two main types: reversible and irreversible. Reversible heat indicating paint changes color temporarily when exposed to high temperatures, allowing for easy monitoring and detection of heat levels. This type of paint is commonly used in routine maintenance and quality control applications. In contrast, irreversible heat indicating paint permanently changes color when exposed to high temperatures, providing a permanent record of heat exposure. This type of paint is typically used for critical applications where a permanent indication of heat levels is necessary for safety or quality assurance.</p></p>
