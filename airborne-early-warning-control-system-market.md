<p><p>Airborne Early Warning Control System (AEWCS) has different market types, including Multispectral and Hyperspectral. Multispectral systems use sensors to capture images in multiple bands of the electromagnetic spectrum, providing information on different material types. Hyperspectral systems have sensors that capture a greater number of narrow bands of the electromagnetic spectrum, allowing for more detailed analysis of the composition of materials. Both types of systems are valuable for various applications, such as monitoring environmental changes and detecting military threats.</p></p>
