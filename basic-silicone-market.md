<p><p>The basic silicone market includes four main types: silicone resin, silicone fluid, silicone elastomer, and silane coupling agent. Silicone resin is a highly durable and heat-resistant material used in coatings, adhesives, and electrical applications. Silicone fluid is a lubricating agent with exceptional thermal stability and electrical insulation properties, commonly used in personal care products and automotive fluids. Silicone elastomer is a flexible and elastic material utilized in various industries, such as healthcare and construction. Silane coupling agent acts as a bonding agent in composite materials, improving their adhesion and strength.</p></p>
