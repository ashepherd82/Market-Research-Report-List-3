<p><p>Outdoor backpacks and rucksacks come in three main types based on their frame design. Frameless backpacks are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for short hikes and everyday use. Internal-frame backpacks have a built-in support system that hugs the wearer's body for better balance and weight distribution, suitable for rugged terrain and heavy loads. External-frame backpacks feature a visible frame that keeps the pack away from the wearer's body, ideal for carrying bulky and heavy gear on long trips. Each type offers unique advantages to fit various outdoor activities.</p></p>
