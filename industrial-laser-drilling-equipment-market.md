<p><p>Industrial Laser Drilling Equipment Market Types refer to two main categories of laser drilling equipment used in industrial applications. Pulsed Fiber Lasers operate by emitting short, high-energy pulses of light, making them suitable for drilling precise and small holes in materials. On the other hand, Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers emit a continuous beam of light, making them ideal for drilling larger holes at a faster rate. Both types of lasers have their own strengths and are used in various industrial sectors for drilling purposes.</p></p>
