<p><p>Ready Mixed Concrete (RMX) is a type of concrete that is manufactured in a batch plant according to a set recipe and then delivered to a construction site in a transit mixer truck. Transit Mixed Concrete is the most common type of RMX, where the concrete is mixed on the way to the site. Shrink Mixed Concrete involves partial mixing at the plant and finishing at the site. Central Mixed Concrete is fully mixed at the plant and then transported to the site.</p></p>
