<p><p>The 3D Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) machine market can be categorized into Large FOV (Field of View) and Medium FOV machines. Large FOV machines are capable of capturing detailed 3D images of larger areas such as the entire jaw or skull, making them ideal for oral surgery and orthodontic procedures. On the other hand, Medium FOV machines are designed for capturing images of specific regions like individual teeth or small sections of the jaw, making them suitable for general dental procedures and implant planning.</p></p>
