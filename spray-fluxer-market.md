<p><p>Spray fluxer market types can be categorized as stationary, reciprocating, and standalone. Stationary fluxers are fixed in place and typically used for high volume production. Reciprocating fluxers move back and forth along the workpiece to ensure even coverage. Standalone fluxers are portable units that can be easily moved around the production floor. Each type of fluxer offers unique benefits and is suited to different production environments based on factors such as space, volume, and flexibility requirements.</p></p>
