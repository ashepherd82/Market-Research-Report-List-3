<p><p>Synthetic ruby market is classified into three main types based on the methods used in the production process. The Flame Melting Method involves melting aluminum oxide powder with chromium in a flame to create synthetic rubies. The Flux Method uses a chemical flux to create the synthetic gemstone by dissolving aluminum oxide and chromium oxide in a high temperature environment. The Hydrothermal Method involves growing synthetic rubies in a high-pressure water solution. Each method has its own advantages and is preferred for different applications in the market.</p></p>
