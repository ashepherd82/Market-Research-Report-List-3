<p><p>Micro guide catheters are medical devices used in minimally invasive procedures to guide other medical instruments, such as wires or stents, through blood vessels. There are two main types of micro guide catheters - diversion micro guide catheters and wire micro guide catheters. Diversion micro guide catheters are designed to divert and redirect the wire or other instruments to reach targeted areas within the body. Wire micro guide catheters, on the other hand, are used primarily to guide wires through tortuous vessels or complex anatomies with precision and control.</p></p>
