<p><ul><li>Acid-based Staining</li><li>Water-based Staining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Colored concrete is available in various types, including acid-based staining and water-based staining. Acid-based staining involves a chemical reaction between the acid and the minerals in the concrete, creating unique, variegated colors. On the other hand, water-based staining is a more environmentally friendly and user-friendly option, as it does not produce harmful fumes and is easier to clean up. Both types of staining provide a wide range of color options and can enhance the aesthetic appeal of concrete surfaces.</p></p>
