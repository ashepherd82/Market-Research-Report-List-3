<p><ul><li>Water-based Fluid</li><li>Foam-based Fluid</li><li>Gelled Oil-based Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic fracturing fluids market is segmented into three main types - Water-based fluid, Foam-based fluid, and Gelled Oil-based fluid. Water-based fluids are the most commonly used type, consisting of water mixed with additives like salts and friction reducers. Foam-based fluids contain foaming agents that help in creating foam for fracturing. Gelled oil-based fluids use oil as a base with gelling agents for viscosity. Each type has its own set of advantages and applications in the hydraulic fracturing process.</p></p>
