<p><p>Forklift gearboxes are essential components that transmit power from the motor to the wheels, allowing the forklift to move and operate efficiently. There are two main types of forklift gearboxes: Internal Combustion Gearbox and Electric Gearbox. Internal Combustion Gearboxes are commonly used in diesel or gasoline-powered forklifts, offering high power and torque capabilities. Electric Gearboxes, on the other hand, are used in electric forklifts and provide smooth and quiet operation. Both types play a crucial role in the performance and functionality of forklifts in various industrial applications.</p></p>
