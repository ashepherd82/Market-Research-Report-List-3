<p><ul><li>Acidic</li><li>Basic</li><li>Direct</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Solvent</li><li>Disperse</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic dye and pigment market types include acidic, basic, direct, reactive, solvent, disperse, and others. Acidic dyes are water-soluble and used on protein fibers. Basic dyes are cationic and used on acrylic, nylon, and silk. Direct dyes are water-soluble and used on natural fibers. Reactive dyes form a covalent bond with fibers like cotton. Solvent dyes are soluble in organic solvents. Disperse dyes are used for synthetic fibers. Other types include vat, sulfur, and pigment dyes, each with unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
