<p><p>Solar panel nano coatings refer to thin films applied to solar panels to improve their surface properties. There are two main types of coatings - hydrophilic and hydrophobic. Hydrophilic coatings attract water molecules and help improve the efficiency of solar panels in humid environments by reducing surface tension and increasing light absorption. On the other hand, hydrophobic coatings repel water, making them ideal for areas with heavy rain or snowfall as they prevent water from accumulating on the surface and blocking sunlight.</p></p>
