<p><p>Photonic crystal sensors are classified into different types based on the material used to fabricate the sensor. These types include single-crystal quartz, lead zirconate titanate, lithium niobate, tourmaline, and others. Single-crystal quartz sensors are known for their high sensitivity and stability. Lead zirconate titanate sensors offer excellent piezoelectric properties. Lithium niobate sensors have unique electro-optic properties. Tourmaline sensors are known for their pyroelectric capabilities. Other types of photonic crystal sensors may use different materials and have varying characteristics and applications.</p></p>
