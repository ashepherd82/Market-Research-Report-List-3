<p><p>Silicone rubber heating pads come in two main types: round heating pads and rectangular heating pads. Round heating pads are typically used for applications that require a circular heating surface, such as on pipes or drums. Rectangular heating pads, on the other hand, are more commonly used for flat or square surfaces, like on flat tanks or surfaces where a more uniform distribution of heat is needed. Both types of heating pads are popular in industrial and commercial applications for their flexibility, durability, and efficiency.</p></p>
