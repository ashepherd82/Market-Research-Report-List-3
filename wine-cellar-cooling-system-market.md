<p><p>Wine cellar cooling systems come in three main types: Self-contained cooling systems are compact and easy to install, perfect for smaller wine cellars. Split cooling systems separate the condenser and evaporator, allowing for quieter operation and more flexibility in installation. Ducted cooling systems are ideal for larger wine cellars, with the ability to hide the unit outside the cellar for a seamless look. Each type offers different benefits depending on the size and layout of the wine cellar.</p></p>
