<p><p>Concrete sealer market types include Silicate Sealers, which penetrate the surface and chemically react with the concrete to increase strength and reduce dusting. Silane Siloxane Sealers provide excellent water repellency without changing the appearance of the concrete. Acrylic Sealers offer a range of finishes from matte to high gloss and provide UV protection. Epoxy Sealers create a hard, chemical-resistant barrier on the surface. Other sealers on the market may include polyurethanes, polyaspartics, or penetrating sealers tailored for specific concrete sealing needs.</p></p>
