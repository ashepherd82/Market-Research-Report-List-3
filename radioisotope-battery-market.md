<p><strong>The Radioisotope Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radioisotope Battery Market includes two types: Thermal Conversion Type and No-Thermal Conversion Type. The Thermal Conversion Type utilizes the heat generated from radioisotope decay to convert it into electrical energy through thermophotovoltaic or thermoelectric conversion. In contrast, the No-Thermal Conversion Type directly converts the radiation emitted by the radioisotope into electrical energy through betavoltaic or alphavoltaic conversion, without the need for a thermal conversion process. These different types offer alternative approaches for harnessing radioisotope energy for various applications.</p></p>
