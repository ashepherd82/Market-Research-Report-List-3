<p><strong>The Fishmeal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam dried(SD)</li><li>Flame dried(FD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fishmeal can be classified into two main types based on the drying process: Steam dried (SD) and Flame dried (FD). Steam dried fishmeal is produced by cooking and drying fish under high pressure using steam, while Flame dried fishmeal is produced by cooking and drying fish using direct flames. SD fishmeal is known for its higher protein content and better nutrient retention, making it suitable for animal feed. On the other hand, FD fishmeal has a stronger flavor and is often used in aquaculture and as a soil fertilizer.</p></p>
