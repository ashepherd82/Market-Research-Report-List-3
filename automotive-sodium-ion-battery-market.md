<p><p>Automotive Sodium-ion Battery Market consists of two main types: Sodium-Salt batteries (also known as Zebra batteries) and Sodium-Sulfur batteries. Sodium-Salt batteries use a molten salt mixture as the electrolyte, providing high energy density. Sodium-Sulfur batteries operate at high temperatures and use sulfur as the cathode, offering a high specific energy. Both types of batteries are suitable for electric vehicles due to their high energy storage capacity and potential for improved performance and efficiency in automotive applications.</p></p>
