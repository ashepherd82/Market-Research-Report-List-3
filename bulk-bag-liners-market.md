<p><p>Bulk bag liners are available in various types such as LLPE, LLDPE, ULDPE, PE/NYLON/EVOH/PE, PE/FOIL/PE, and PE/METALOCENE/PE. LLPE liners are known for their high tensile strength and impact resistance, while LLDPE liners offer good flexibility and elongation properties. ULDPE liners are favored for their excellent moisture barrier properties. PE/NYLON/EVOH/PE liners provide a high barrier to oxygen and moisture. PE/FOIL/PE liners offer exceptional barrier properties against gases and UV light. PE/METALOCENE/PE liners are known for their superior sealing strength and puncture resistance.</p></p>
