<p><p>Industrial X-ray CT systems are widely used for various applications, with two primary types being Micro CT and Nano CT. Micro CT systems have a resolution in the micrometer range and are typically used for imaging small samples such as electronics and medical devices. On the other hand, Nano CT systems have a resolution in the nanometer range, making them ideal for imaging at the atomic and molecular level. Both types of systems play a crucial role in industrial research and quality control processes.</p></p>
