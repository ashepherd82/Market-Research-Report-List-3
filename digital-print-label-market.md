<p><p>The digital print label market includes various types such as wet glued labels, pressure sensitive/self adhesive labels, liner-less labels, multi-part barcode labels, in-mold labels, and shrink sleeve labels. Wet glued labels are applied using adhesive, while pressure sensitive labels have a built-in adhesive that sticks when pressure is applied. Liner-less labels do not have backing paper, multi-part barcode labels have multiple layers, in-mold labels are applied during manufacturing, and shrink sleeve labels wrap around containers and shrink to fit when heated. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in the digital print label market.</p></p>
