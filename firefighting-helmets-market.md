<p><p>Firefighting helmets can be categorized into two main types: full face coverage and half face helmet. Full face coverage helmets provide the most protection as they cover the entire face, including the eyes and ears. On the other hand, half face helmets provide protection only to the top of the head, leaving the face exposed. Both types are designed to protect firefighters from heat, flames, falling debris, and other hazards while allowing for clear communication and visibility during emergency situations.</p></p>
