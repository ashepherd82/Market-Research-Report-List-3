<p><p>High Performance Admixture for Concrete market can be categorized into various types based on their functions. Water Reducing Agents are used to reduce the water content in the concrete mix, improving workability and strength. Waterproofing Agents help make the concrete impermeable to water. Accelerating Agents speed up the setting and curing process. Air-entraining Agents are used to introduce air bubbles into the mix, increasing freeze-thaw resistance. Retarding Agents slow down the setting time. Other types of admixtures include plasticizers, corrosion inhibitors, and shrinkage reducers.</p></p>
