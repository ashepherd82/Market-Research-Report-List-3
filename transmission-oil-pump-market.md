<p><p>Transmission oil pumps are essential components in vehicles that help to circulate oil through the transmission system. There are two main types of transmission oil pumps in the market: fixed displacement pumps and variable displacement pumps. Fixed displacement pumps deliver a consistent flow of oil regardless of engine speed, while variable displacement pumps adjust their output based on the engine's demand. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different vehicle applications based on the specific requirements of the transmission system.</p></p>
