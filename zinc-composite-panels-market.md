<p><p>Zinc composite panels are categorized based on their thickness into three main types: <3mm, 3-5mm, and >5mm. The <3mm panels are thinner and often used in interior design applications for their lightweight and flexibility. The 3-5mm panels strike a balance between thickness and weight, making them suitable for both interior and exterior use. The >5mm panels are thicker and heavier, providing enhanced durability and strength for building facades and high-traffic areas. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
