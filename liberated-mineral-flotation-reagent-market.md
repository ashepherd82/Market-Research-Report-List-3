<p><p>Liberated mineral flotation reagent market can be categorized into different types such as flotation collectors, flotation depressants, flotation activators, and others. Flotation collectors are chemicals that enhance the hydrophobicity of mineral surfaces, allowing for better attachment to air bubbles. Flotation depressants inhibit the floatation of certain minerals, while flotation activators promote the floatation of specific minerals. Other reagents in this market may include pH regulators, modifiers, and frothers, which play different roles in the flotation process.</p></p>
