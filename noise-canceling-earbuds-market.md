<p><strong>The Noise Canceling Earbuds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Noise-Cancelling Earbuds</li><li>Passive Noise-Cancelling Earbuds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Noise-Cancelling Earbuds use built-in microphones to pick up external noise and then produce sound waves that are the exact opposite to cancel out that noise. These earbuds are ideal for blocking out loud environments such as airplanes or busy offices. Passive Noise-Canceling Earbuds, on the other hand, block out external noise by physically blocking the ear canal with a snug fit and using materials that absorb sound. Both types cater to consumers looking for a more immersive and distraction-free listening experience.</p></p>
