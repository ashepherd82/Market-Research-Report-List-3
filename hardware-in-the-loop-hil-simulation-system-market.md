<p><p>Closed Hardware-in-the-Loop Simulation systems are proprietary and rely on specific hardware and software components from a single provider. This limits customization and integration with other systems. On the other hand, Open Hardware-in-the-Loop Simulation systems use standard interfaces and protocols, allowing for greater flexibility and interoperability with different components and systems. Users have the freedom to mix and match hardware and software from various vendors, enabling tailored solutions to meet specific testing requirements.</p></p>
