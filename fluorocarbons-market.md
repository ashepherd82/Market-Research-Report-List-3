<p><p>Fluorocarbons are a type of organofluorine compound that contain carbon and fluorine atoms. They are categorized into different market types based on their chemical structure. Perfluoroalkanes are fully fluorinated compounds, Fluoroalkenes contain double bonds between carbon and fluorine atoms, Fluoroalkynes have triple bonds, and Perfluoroaromatic compounds contain aromatic rings with fully fluorinated carbon atoms. Each type of fluorocarbon has unique properties and applications in various industries including electronics, aerospace, and healthcare.</p></p>
