<p><p>Specialty film market includes various types such as stretch film, shrink film, barrier film, conductive film, and others. Stretch film is used for wrapping pallets and securing loads during transportation. Shrink film is used for packaging products by shrinking when heat is applied. Barrier film is used for protecting food and pharmaceutical products from moisture and oxygen. Conductive film is used in electronic devices to dissipate static electricity. Other specialty films include release films, lamination films, and safety films, each with unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
