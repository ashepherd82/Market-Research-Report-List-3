<p><p>Polyamide hinges come in three main types: 90°, 180°, and 270°. The 90° hinges allow for a right angle rotation, making them suitable for applications where limited movement is required. The 180° hinges have a wider range of motion, allowing for a full half-circle rotation. The 270° hinges offer the largest range of motion, allowing for almost a complete circle rotation. Each type of hinge has its own unique advantages and applications, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
