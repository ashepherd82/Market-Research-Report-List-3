<p><strong>The Geosynthetics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geotextiles</li><li>Geomembranes</li><li>Geogrids</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geosynthetics are materials used in civil engineering applications for enhancing the performance of soil, rock, or other geotechnical materials. Geotextiles are fabrics used to improve soil stability, filtration, and drainage. Geomembranes are impermeable sheets used for containment applications such as landfill liners and pond liners. Geogrids are grid-like structures used for soil reinforcement and retaining wall construction. Other types of geosynthetics include geocomposites, geonets, and geocells, each serving specific functions in various civil engineering projects.</p></p>
