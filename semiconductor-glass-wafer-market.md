Quartz wafers are popular due to their high purity, transparency, and excellent thermal properties. Fused silica wafers offer low thermal expansion and high transmission of ultraviolet light. Each type of glass wafer caters to different semiconductor manufacturing needs, providing unique properties for specific applications within the industry.</p></p>
