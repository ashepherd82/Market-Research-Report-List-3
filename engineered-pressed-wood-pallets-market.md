<p><p>Engineered pressed wood pallets come in two main market types: nestable pallets and rackable pallets. Nestable pallets are designed to stack within each other when not in use, saving space during storage and transportation. Rackable pallets, on the other hand, are designed to be used in pallet racking systems for easy access to goods. Both types offer durability, cost-effectiveness, and sustainability benefits, making them popular choices in various industries for their efficient storage and transportation solutions.</p></p>
