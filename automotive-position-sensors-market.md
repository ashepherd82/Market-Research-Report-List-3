<p><strong>The Automotive Position Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-axis</li><li>Angular</li><li>Linear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive position sensors market is segmented into three main types: multi-axis, angular, and linear. Multi-axis sensors are capable of measuring position in multiple directions simultaneously, making them ideal for complex motion systems. Angular sensors measure the rotational position of components like steering wheels or throttle pedals. Linear sensors, on the other hand, measure linear displacement along a single axis, making them useful for applications like throttle position sensing. Each type of sensor plays a crucial role in enabling precise control and feedback in automotive systems.</p></p>
