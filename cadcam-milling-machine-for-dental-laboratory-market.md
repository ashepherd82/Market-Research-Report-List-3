<p><ul><li>Subtractive Processes</li><li>Additive Processes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CAD/CAM milling machines for dental laboratories utilize either subtractive or additive processes. Subtractive processes involve milling the dental prosthesis from a solid block of material, eliminating unwanted parts until the desired form is achieved. This method is known for its precision and accuracy. On the other hand, additive processes involve building the prosthesis layer by layer using materials like resins or metals. This technique allows for intricate designs and customization. Dentists can choose the suitable market type based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
