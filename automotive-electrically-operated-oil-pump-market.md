<p><strong>The Automotive Electrically Operated Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Pump</li><li>Separate Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive electrically operated oil pumps can be categorized into two main types: integrated pump and separate pump. Integrated pumps are built into the engine and are typically smaller and more compact, providing a space-saving solution. On the other hand, separate pumps are installed externally and are usually larger in size. Separate pumps offer more flexibility in terms of installation and servicing. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in maintaining proper lubrication and cooling of the engine in vehicles.</p></p>
