<p><p>Flame retardant fiber market consists of two main types: staple fiber and long fiber. Staple fiber is short in length and is commonly used in nonwoven fabrics and textiles due to its excellent thermal insulation and flame retardant properties. On the other hand, long fiber is longer in length and is typically used in applications where strength and durability are required, such as in ropes and cables. Both types of fibers play a crucial role in providing fire protection in various industries.</p></p>
