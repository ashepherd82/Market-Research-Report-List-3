<p><p>Semicon Etchants Market involves two main types of etching agents: Wet Etching Agents and Dry Etching Agents. Wet etching agents involve immersing the semiconductor material in a liquid solution to selectively remove material, while dry etching agents involve using plasma or gas to etch the material. Wet etching is suitable for high selectivity and lower aspect ratios, while dry etching is preferred for higher aspect ratios and better control over the etching process. Both methods are essential for the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
