<p><p>The Smart Battery Swap Station market includes two main types: Chassis Battery Swap Mode and Sub-Box Battery Replacement Mode. In Chassis Battery Swap Mode, the entire battery unit is replaced within the chassis of the vehicle, allowing for a quick and efficient swap process. On the other hand, Sub-Box Battery Replacement Mode involves replacing individual battery modules within a sub-box unit, providing more flexibility in terms of maintenance and service. Both modes cater to different needs and preferences within the smart battery swap industry.</p></p>
