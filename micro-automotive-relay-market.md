<p><p>The micro automotive relay market consists of two main types: plug-in relays and PCB relays. Plug-in relays are designed to easily connect and disconnect from a socket, making them convenient for replacement or maintenance. PCB relays are soldered directly onto a circuit board, offering a more secure and stable connection. Both types of relays play a crucial role in controlling various electrical components in vehicles, such as lights, fans, and motors, contributing to the overall efficiency and performance of automotive systems.</p></p>
