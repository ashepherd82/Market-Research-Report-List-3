<p><p>Thyristor Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC) in Blocking Mode is used to increase power transfer capability and voltage stability in the power system. Bypass Mode provides flexible control over reactive power compensation. Capacitive Boost Mode enhances power transfer capability by inserting capacitive reactance. Inductive Boost Mode increases power transfer capability by inserting inductive reactance. These market types cater to different needs of power system operators, such as improving transmission efficiency, voltage control, and stability, by dynamically adjusting the impedance of the transmission line.</p></p>
