<p><p>The E-mountain bike market is divided into three main types based on the motor location: Front Hub Motor, Rear Hub Motor, and Mid-Drive Motor. Front Hub Motor bikes have the motor located in the front wheel hub, providing easier handling and maintenance. Rear Hub Motor bikes have the motor in the rear wheel hub, giving a more natural riding feel. Mid-Drive Motor bikes have the motor positioned in the center of the bike, offering better balance and efficiency by utilizing the bike's gears. Each type caters to different preferences and riding styles.</p></p>
