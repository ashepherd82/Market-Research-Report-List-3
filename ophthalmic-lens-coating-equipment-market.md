<p><p>Ophthalmic Lens Coating Equipment Market offers various types of coating methods such as Dip Coating and Spin Coating. Dip Coating involves immersing the lens into a coating solution and then withdrawing it at a controlled rate to create a uniform coating. Spin Coating, on the other hand, involves placing the lens on a spinning platform and applying the coating solution, which spreads out evenly due to centrifugal force. Both methods are widely used in the ophthalmic industry for applying coatings to lenses.</p></p>
