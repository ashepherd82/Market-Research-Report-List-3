<p><p>Near-infrared spectroscopy (NIR) is a technology used to analyze the composition of materials by measuring the absorption of light in the near-infrared range. NIR analyzers, such as FT-NIR (Fourier Transform-NIR) and NIR interferometers, are two types of devices used in this market. FT-NIR analyzers employ interferometers to divide light into different wavelengths, enabling the measurement of the light's absorption by the sample. On the other hand, NIR interferometers directly measure the interference pattern of light, allowing for quick and accurate analysis of various substances.</p></p>
