<p><p>ArF resist, short for Argon fluoride resist, is a photosensitive material used in photolithography processes for semiconductor manufacturing. It is used as a coating on silicon wafers to transfer circuit patterns from photomasks to the wafer surface during fabrication. The ArF resist is known for its high resolution, good etch selectivity, and low line edge roughness, making it ideal for producing high-density integrated circuits.</p><p>The ArF Resist Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period. <p><p>ArF resist market can be divided into two main types: ArF Dry Resist and ArF Immersion Resist. ArF Dry Resist is used for single layer patterning processes, while ArF Immersion Resist is designed for multi-layer patterning processes. Both types of resist are used in semiconductor manufacturing for creating intricate patterns on silicon wafers. ArF Dry Resist offers high resolution and good etch resistance, while ArF Immersion Resist provides improved resolution and depth of focus for advanced lithography processes.</p></p>
